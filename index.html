Newest Project

Take a look at my newest creation, and be impressed by the innovative design, skilled execution and attention to detail that went into making it. It's a representation of my passion for creating and my commitment to excellence. Explore it and let me know what you think.

Medical Scale

Medical Scale, equipped with an Arduino Nano microcontroller and featuring Bioelectrical Impedance Analysis for body composition calculation, a Loadcell sensor for weight measurement, and a VL53L1X sensor for height measurement. Connected to a Raspberry Pi LCD and mobile app, our scale makes it easy for users to track and monitor their health results.

Baby Scale

A baby scale utilizing ESP32 microcontroller and low energy Bluetooth technology for efficient power usage and integrated with an Android app for monitoring the baby's weight progress through provided graphs.

Blue Lumina

An IoT system for measuring water temperature, total dissolved solids, pH, and turbidity, integrated with the Blynk platform.

Face Mask and Temperature Detector

Better Plant

Cashier Point of Sales
